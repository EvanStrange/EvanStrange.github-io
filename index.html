Hi, I'm Evan Strange...

I'm a third year Computing Systems Engineering Technology student at CNA in St. John's, Newfoundland. I have had an interest in IT for as long as I can remember...

My mother went back to school to do an IT course shortly after I was born, therefore some of my earliest memories are playing brain-teaser games designed for young people on Windows 98. By age 12 I was assembling my own Windows PC from parts purchased locally, in order to better run Guild Wars. My parents were extremely supportive from a young age about my passion in IT.

Throughout grade school I began experimenting with doodling, paint and colors. I was fascinated how such simple color schemes could be so visually pleasing. Eventually, my two hobbies collided as I took a course in highschool teaching the fundamentals of HTML and CSS.

The first two years of my three-year program focus mainly on programming fundamentals and object-oriented programming using Java. While this is incredibly useful, my skills with front-end development set me apart from most of my class.

I'm new to this field and seeking experience.
